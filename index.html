Methodology: scores derived from a proprietary index of plantable backdrops, menu words ending in "-ata" or "-ito", proximity to a coastal aesthetic, and whether the venue could plausibly feature in a "things to do on the Central Coast" reel. A 12-point bonus is applied to any venue where the seating requires getting up from a low surface without incident, on the basis that Gen Z can do this freely and without consequence, which is one of several superior thoracic mobility advantages they hold over the two people who made this page, both of whom are 40 and have opinions about mobility generally. Peer reviewed by no one. Confidence interval: vibes.
